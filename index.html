Data Deletion and Retention

The Service Provider does not collect or store any personal user data on its own servers.

Android Users: The only data transmitted is managed by Google Play Games services for game functionalities (leaderboards and achievements). Game data is kept on Google's servers even after the application is uninstalled. If you wish to delete it, you can do so directly via the Google Play Games app on your Android device. To delete your game data (scores and achievements), follow these simple steps:

  • Open the Google Play Games app;
  • Go to Settings;
  • Select "Delete account and game data";
  • Find and select ChirpQuiz from the list of games;
  • Confirm the deletion.
For all other data stored locally on your device by the app, it will be automatically deleted when you uninstall the Application.

iOS Users: All data is stored locally. Uninstalling the app deletes all data.
